About Me

I'm a CS student at UIUC who enjoys building clean, responsive web apps. Recently, I've been practicing modern HTML5, SCSS, and vanilla JavaScript to sharpen my frontend skills.

Outside class, I like to work out at Planet Fitness, study at Grainger Engineering Library, and unwind by exploring the Arboretum. These places are also featured below.

For this MP1, I focused on user-friendly interactions: a sticky navbar with scroll indicator, a responsive carousel, a parallax section, and a modal — all without UI libraries.
